Working with PathCheck and Other Volunteers

PathCheck is a user privacy centered company that is working on exposure notification and vaccination apps to help stop the spread of COVID-19. I recently worked on a recommendation for PathCheck launching an exposure notification app in Lousiana. Check that out over here. It mostly highlighted the benefit of using emotionally connected ads to connect with the user and encourage them to download the app.

PathCheck has decided to launch a vaccination app. This vaccination app works by first scanning the QR code on the vaccine and verifying its authenticity and dose number. Then the vaccine app asks if you are experiencing any symptoms and how severe they are. After the first dose, if a second dose is required, then you would go back to the app and repeat the process. The app also has a FAQ and a contact section to help the user. This app assists users by recording the vaccinations and keeping track of their symptoms

The process of creating an app, especially with such strict privacy rules, is not easy. PathCheck employees are working with volunteers to expedite the process of releasing the app and doing their part to help stop the spread of COVID-19. I am currently researching different applications that keep track of vaccinations, such as the Vaccine Administration Management System (VAMS) and VTrckS. Using features from these current vaccine trackers can help PathCheck enhance their app and experience. I am also working with Figma to help create mockups for PathChecks exposure notifications and vaccine apps. PathCheck is working with everyone from content writers to UI designers to help make their apps a reality.
